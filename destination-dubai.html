Dubai Tours

Dubai - Where East Meets West

Dubai perfectly complements your East African safari with its unique blend of ultra-modern luxury and traditional Arabian culture. Often combined with Kenyan or Tanzanian safaris, Dubai offers world-class shopping, futuristic architecture like the Burj Khalifa, thrilling desert adventures, and pristine beaches. This cosmopolitan city provides an exciting contrast to the wilderness of Africa with its state-of-the-art attractions and luxury experiences.

Dubai Highlights

Desert Safaris

Dune bashing, camel rides, and traditional Bedouin experiences.

Burj Khalifa

The world's tallest building with observation decks at 124 & 148 floors.

Old Dubai

Explore traditional souks, Dubai Creek, and the historic Al Fahidi district.

Dhow Cruises

Traditional Arabian boat cruises with dinner and entertainment.

Best Time to Visit Dubai

November to March: Peak season with pleasant temperatures (20-25°C) - ideal for outdoor activities.

April to October: Very hot with temperatures exceeding 40°C - best for indoor attractions and beach time.

Practical Information

Visa

Visa on arrival for many nationalities. Check requirements before travel.

Currency

Dirham (AED). Credit cards accepted everywhere.

Language

Arabic and English are official languages.

Climate

Desert climate - hot summers, mild winters, low humidity.
