Rwanda Safaris

Rwanda - Land of a Thousand Hills

Rwanda is East Africa's fastest-developing safari destination, renowned for its mountain gorilla trekking experiences in Volcanoes National Park. The country has invested heavily in conservation and tourism infrastructure, making it an easy and rewarding destination. Beyond gorillas, Rwanda offers golden monkey tracking, chimpanzee trekking in Nyungwe Forest, and relaxation by Lake Kivu. The clean, safe capital Kigali provides an excellent starting point for your safari adventure.

Rwanda Highlights

Volcanoes National Park

Mountain gorilla trekking and golden monkey encounters.

Nyungwe Forest National Park

Chimpanzee tracking and canopy walk above the rainforest.

Lake Kivu

Relaxing beaches and island retreats on Rwanda's largest lake.

Kigali

Africa's cleanest city with excellent restaurants and museums.

Best Time to Visit Rwanda

June to September: Dry season - best for gorilla trekking and chimpanzee tracking.

December to February: Short dry season - pleasant weather for all activities.

Practical Information

Visa

eVisa available online for most nationalities.

Currency

Rwandan Franc (RWF). USD widely accepted in tourist areas.

Language

Kinyarwanda, English, and French are official languages.

Climate

Tropical highland climate - mild temperatures year-round.
