Tanzania Safaris

Tanzania - Home of the Serengeti

Tanzania is the ultimate safari destination featuring the world-renowned Serengeti National Park, the Ngorongoro Crater (a UNESCO World Heritage site), Mount Kilimanjaro (Africa's highest peak), and the exotic island of Zanzibar. The annual wildebeest migration traverses these lands, creating one of nature's greatest spectacles. From the calving season in February to the dramatic river crossings in July, Tanzania offers an unparalleled safari experience.

Tanzania Highlights

Serengeti National Park

Endless plains hosting the Great Migration and abundant predators year-round.

Ngorongoro Crater

A unique caldera ecosystem with exceptional wildlife density.

Mount Kilimanjaro

Ascend Africa's highest peak through diverse ecological zones.

Selous Game Reserve

Vast wilderness area with boat safaris and walking adventures.

Best Time to Visit Tanzania

June to October: Dry season - peak season for Serengeti and Ngorongoro. Great for wildebeest river crossings.

January to March: Short dry season - excellent for calving in the southern Serengeti.

Practical Information

Visa

eVisa available online for most nationalities.

Currency

Tanzanian Shilling (TZS). USD widely accepted.

Language

English and Swahili are official languages.

Climate

Tropical with coastal and inland variations. 20-30°C typical.
