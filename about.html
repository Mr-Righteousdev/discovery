About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate Kenyan guides, Africa Safari Discovery began with a simple mission: to share the authentic beauty and wildlife of East Africa with the world. Starting with just two safari vehicles and a deep knowledge of the land, we have grown into one of Kenya's most trusted tour operators while maintaining our commitment to personalized service and conservation.

Our founders, born and raised in Nairobi, grew up exploring the national parks of Kenya and Tanzania. Their vision was to create safari experiences that not only showcase the incredible wildlife but also support local communities and conservation efforts. Today, we employ over 50 local guides and staff across East Africa, all sharing the same passion for wildlife and hospitality.
