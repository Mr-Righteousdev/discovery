Uganda Safaris

Uganda - The Pearl of Africa

Uganda is a land of incredible biodiversity and natural beauty. Home to endangered mountain gorillas in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest and Mgahinga Gorilla National Park, chimpanzees in Kibale Forest, and the source of the Nile River at Jinja. Uganda offers unique primate tracking experiences alongside classic savanna safaris in Queen Elizabeth National Park and Murchison Falls. The country's compact size allows for diverse experiences in a single trip.

Uganda Highlights

Bwindi Impenetrable Forest

Habitat of the endangered mountain gorillas.

Queen Elizabeth National Park

Tree-climbing lions and boat safaris on the Kazinga Channel.

Murchison Falls

The dramatic Nile River as it squeezes through a 7m gap.

Kibale Forest

Excellent chimpanzee tracking and 13 other primate species.

Best Time to Visit Uganda

June to September: Dry season - ideal for gorilla trekking and general wildlife viewing.

December to February: Short dry season - another good window for primate tracking.

Practical Information

Visa

eVisa available online for most nationalities.

Currency

Ugandan Shilling (UGX). USD widely accepted.

Language

English is the official language.

Climate

Tropical with relatively consistent temperatures year-round.
